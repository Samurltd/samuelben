Human Resources Analytics Dashboard

The Human Resources Dashboard provides an end-to-end analytical view of workforce performance, demographics, and operational efficiency. Built using Tableau, it enables HR teams, business leaders, and analysts to gain actionable insights from employee data in real time.

Overview Page

This interactive page offers a high-level summary of workforce trends and organizational distribution. It presents:

  • Employee Overview: Total active employees, hires, and terminations to monitor workforce growth.
  • Departmental Breakdown: Insights into hires and terminations per department to track retention.
  • Demographics Analysis: Gender, education, and age segmentation for diversity tracking.
  • Income Insights: Salary trends by education and gender, and age-to-salary correlation for pay analysis.
  • Location Map: Visual workforce distribution between HQ and branch offices.

Employee Details Page

The detailed dashboard view provides granular employee-level insights that support effective workforce management. It includes:

  • Dynamic Filters: Filter by job title, department, salary range, education, and employment status.
  • Individual Employee Profiles: Displays employee demographics, salary, and job details in a searchable format.
  • Employment Timeline: Visualizes tenure length, hire and termination dates for retention analysis.
  • Data-Driven Transparency: Provides HR leaders with comprehensive workforce visibility in one view.

Tools Used: Tableau, Excel, SQL, and Python
